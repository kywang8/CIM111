March 18, 2018

Wide Receiver Cordarrelle Patterson was traded from the Oakland Raiders to the New England Patriots. New England swapped their fifth-round pick in this year's draft for Oakland's sixth-round pick.

March 17, 2018

Runningback Marshawn Lynch will re-join the Oakland Raiders for the 2018 season under new HC Jon Gruden. He is set to make $4 million in the upcoming season. Lynch came out of retirement last year to play for his hometeam after a successful career with the Seattle Seahawks. He is affectionately referred to as "Beast Mode," and is known for his antics on and off the field, including funny interviews and a love for Skittles.
