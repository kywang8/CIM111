As a member of UMTV's SportsDesk program, I crewed the show and worked as an on-air analyst for the Head to Head segment. Head to Head is similar to First Take with Stephen A. Smith, Max Kellerman, and Molly Qerim, only, of course, much shorter. The segment typically lasted 2-3 minutes and consisted of two analysts debating about some sports topic while moderated by a third party.
