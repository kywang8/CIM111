Italy

Italy is full of vibrant cities, delicious food, and historical landmarks. Visit Florence for its fantastic food and get swept up in the beautiful city, or take a few days to enjoy a world famous gondola ride through the waterways of Venice. Explore the Colosseum of Rome and walk in the footsteps of gladiators, or climb the steps of the Leaning Tower of Pisa.

Five Things To Do In Italy

  1. Explore the Colosseum of Rome
  2. Take a gondola ride in Venice
  3. Climb the Leaning Tower of Pisa
  4. See Michelangelo's David at the Accademia Gallery in Florence
  5. Try Ristorante Toto's steak in Florence
